CleanHacks - Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

Projevity Pty Ltd ("we", "us", "our") respects your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use the CleanHacks app.

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: If you choose to create an account, we collect your email address and an encrypted password. Anonymous users do not provide this information.
  • User-Generated Content: Photos, videos, and text you upload when posting a cleaning hack or review.
  • Camera & Media: When you use the "Snap to Clean" feature, the app requires access to your device's camera or photo library.
  • Device & Usage Data: Timezone, device type, crash logs, and app interaction data to improve our services.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • AI Processing: Images captured via the "Snap to Clean" feature are sent to third-party AI providers (Google Gemini) strictly for the purpose of analyzing the image to provide cleaning solutions.
  • App Functionality: To display your saved shopping lists, user profile, and community posts.
  • Support: To respond to bug reports and support tickets.

3. Third-Party Services

We utilize third-party services that may collect information used to identify you:

  • Supabase: For database hosting and secure authentication.
  • Google Gemini API: For image processing and AI generation.
  • AdMob (Google): For displaying advertisements. These providers operate under their own privacy policies.

4. Data Deletion & Your Rights

You have the right to request the deletion of your personal data.

  • In-App Deletion: You can permanently delete your account and associated data (including posted hacks and reviews) at any time by navigating to Profile Settings > Delete Account within the app. This action is irreversible.

5. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures (including secure Edge Functions and encrypted databases) to protect your data. However, no electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. Changes will be reflected within the app.

7. Contact Us

If you have questions about your privacy, contact us via the in-app support feature or at support@projevity.com.au.
